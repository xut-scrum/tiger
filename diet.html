Diet Information

Tigers are carnivores and apex predators. Their main diet is large and medium sized ungulates, but they can be opportunistic.

Typical prey

  • Large and medium ungulates such as deer and wild boar
  • Smaller animals (for example birds, porcupines, fish) when available
  • Sometimes livestock near settlements if natural prey is scarce
  • Occasionally vegetation, fruit, and minerals as supplements

How they hunt

  • Ambush strategy: they use cover, crouch low, and wait for a close opportunity.
  • They may wait near watering holes, especially in hot weather.
  • If the prey notices them too early, they often stop the chase rather than running long distances.
