Scientific Name and Classification

Scientific name

  • Species: Panthera tigris
  • Genus: Panthera
  • Family: Felidae (cats)

Short taxonomy history

  • In 1758, Carl Linnaeus described the tiger and used the name Felis tigris.
  • In 1929, Reginald Innes Pocock placed the species in the genus Panthera as Panthera tigris.

Subspecies

  • Tigers are traditionally classified into nine subspecies.
  • Some approaches recognize two broader groups: mainland Asian tigers and island tigers of the Sunda Islands.
  • This site uses common names like Bengal tiger and Siberian tiger for clarity.
