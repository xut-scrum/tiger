Habitat

Tigers live mainly in forests. Across their range this can mean coniferous and temperate forests in the far north, and moist tropical forests in South and Southeast Asia.

Where tigers live today

  • As of 2022, tigers live in less than 7% of their historic distribution.
  • Today their range is scattered across the Indian subcontinent, the Indochinese Peninsula, Sumatra, northeastern China, and the Russian Far East.

What tigers need from a habitat

  • Vegetation cover for ambush hunting
  • A stable prey base, especially large and medium sized ungulates
  • Access to water (tigers are strong swimmers)
  • Enough space for home ranges and movement corridors
