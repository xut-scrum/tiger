Bengal tiger

Short notes

  • Bengal tigers are described as having a yellow to light orange coat with dark stripes and a white underside.
  • They live in several landscapes across the Indian subcontinent, with India having the largest extent of global tiger habitat.
  • They are carnivores that prefer hunting large ungulates, but can also take smaller prey and sometimes livestock.
